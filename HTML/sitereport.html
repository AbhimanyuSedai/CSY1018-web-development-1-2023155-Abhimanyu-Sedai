Site Report

Introduction

In this site report, i will be talking about my journey of creating this website. The purpose of this website is to illustrate my potential and skills.
At the beginning of this project, I was quite frighten of the work i had to do. At first, I made a plan where i seperated work according to its
importance. I also decided what elements to add or remove from my web pages and did my research accordingly.

Design

Designing this website was one of the most challenging thing to overcome in this assignment. Initally i was planning to go for a yellowish white
background but after going through some websites, i found black background appealing. Added to that, i also found white font color with some
orange touch was perfect color scheme to go with. Talking about the font, I used Google Font to import the fonts. I used 'Michroma' , 'Rajdhani' and
'Work Sans' for my website as it was looking teckey. I used HTML and CSS to make my website responsive which means it can adjust to different
size resolutions. I also imported various custom icons according to my choice to make my website look more attractive and clean. I also added other
design elements ie hover, animations, transitions etc.

Content

The website contains of several pages containing home-page, portfolio, contact, experience and site-report page. In the Home Page i included a
brief intro about me and my website. I also added some animations and interactive pictures to encourage the visitors. The Portfolio Page contains
some random pictures as assigned. The Contact Page is where the visitors can get in touch with me. There is a form you can fill and leave a messege
or directly contact me through my details mentioned in the page. You can learn my work history from Experience Page. I have also added some
animations to make it more interesting. Lastly, the SiteReport Page provides a comprehensive overview of the website's structure, functionality,
contents and design.

Challenges

One of the main challenge of creating this website was to make it responsive to every screen resolutions. I was stuck in a problem and even web
searches couldnt help. I eventually overcame it by going through every line of my code. The other challenge was to make my website functionally
and visually appealing. I was also in a dilemma to choose a design for my navigation bar. I had to do my nav bar for several times to get my
final design.

Conclusion

Overall, this assignment was very rewarding inspite of its challenges. Not only i have broaden my knowledge about web development but also
have developed problem solving skills. Lastly, this assignment have prepared me for my further obstacles and future assignments in this field.
